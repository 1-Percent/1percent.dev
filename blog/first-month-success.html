First Month
Success

Better than I could have hoped for.

When launching this project recently there was a fantastic amount of discussion underway. I was also really enthusiastic to see people pledge to donate 1% of their income to open source at this very early stage of the project Below are the tweets from Chris and Jeroen:

Made it the other day, forgot to share - https://t.co/e1Qto88QJH ☺️

— Chris Gmyr (@cmgmyr) September 29, 2019

I have decided to follow @ssxio and starting from the 1st of October I will give 1% of my pay check to open source. So give me some suggestions on open source projects you think should make the list. https://t.co/HAGuFQGteQ

— 0.5x developer (@n0x13) September 25, 2019

I was a little taken back by the positive reception the project got but more importantly I do believe it stoked the conversation and made people think about the software they use freely.

Also, I'd initially planned on splitting this months donation out between Composer and PHPUnit but in the end I decided that I'd pick one instead as that gives them a much more sizable donation.
